# 📊 Customer Analytics Dashboard – Zara Sales (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze customer and sales data for Zara. The dashboard provides key insights into revenue distribution, product performance, and business drivers, enabling better data-driven decision-making.

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights
The report includes the following key metrics:
- Total Revenue  
- Total Sales  
- Average Price  
- Total Products  

## 🔍 Key Analysis
- % of Revenue by Product Category (Items)  
- % of Revenue by Product Position 
- % of Revenue by Section (Men/Women)  
- % of Revenue by Promotion  
- % of Revenue by Seasonal Products  

## 🚀 Key Insights
- Certain product categories contribute significantly to overall revenue  
- Product placement plays a crucial role in sales performance  
- Revenue distribution varies across sections and promotional strategies  
- Seasonal and non-seasonal products have balanced contributions
